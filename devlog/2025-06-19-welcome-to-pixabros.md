## Hello, World!

Welcome to the PixaBros devlog! This is where we'll share behind-the-scenes looks at our game development process, announce new releases, and give you a peek at what's cooking.

### Who We Are

We're Furkan and Enes Baytekin — two brothers who make games together. We handle **everything** in the game development process ourselves:

- **Code** — From gameplay systems to shaders
- **2D Art** — Pixel art, illustrations, UI
- **Music & SFX** — Original soundtracks for every game
- **Game Design** — Mechanics, levels, balance
- **Animation** — Bringing characters and worlds to life
- **Polish** — That final 10% that makes a game feel *right*

### What to Expect

In this devlog, you'll find:

- Post-mortems on released games
- Work-in-progress sneak peeks
- Technical deep dives into our tools and workflows
- Game jam retrospective
- And probably some silly stuff too

### Our Games So Far

We've made **14 games** across a wild range of genres — from puzzle platformers to rhythm action, narrative adventures to survivor-likes. Each one taught us something new.
